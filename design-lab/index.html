We deleted the prompt box.

We started building Workprint as a better way to write build-in-public posts.

The first version looked familiar: give AI a pile of work, then ask it to turn that pile into content. It looked reasonable. It was also incomplete.

The blank prompt was making builders do the hardest part all over again: figure out what mattered.

So we removed it. Workprint now reads the evidence your work already leaves behind and brings back a few things worth talking about.

The result isn’t five generic posts. It’s one story that could only belong to the person who did the work.
